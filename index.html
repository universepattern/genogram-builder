How to Draw Your Genogram

1. Spawn Members: Click any gender shape in the bottom legend (e.g. Male (Add)) to spawn a member.
2. Edit Details: The editor opens automatically. Enter details and click Save Changes.
3. Connect Partners: Click one member, select Use as Partner A. Click another, select Use as Partner B. In the Build tab, click Connect Partners.
4. Link Children: Click a parent connection line, select Use as Parent Pair. Click a child node, select Use as Child. Click Link Child.
💡 Drag any shape to arrange. Nodes automatically snap to the grid and avoid overlaps!

📖 Quick Guide

Follow this simple workflow to draw your family tree:

1. Spawn Members Click any gender shape in the bottom legend (e.g. Male (Add)) to place a new member at the center of the screen.
2. Edit Details The editor opens automatically on creation. Type their name, set age/years/medical conditions, and click Save Changes.
3. Connect Partners Click one member, select Use as Partner A. Click their partner, select Use as Partner B. Go to section 2 in the sidebar and click Connect Partners.
4. Link Children Click the connection line between parents, select Use as Parent Pair. Click a child node, select Use as Child. Click Link Child in the sidebar.

💡 Tip: Drag any member on the canvas to rearrange them. They automatically snap to the grid and avoid overlaps!

Welcome to Genogram.studio

An interactive academic tool to build clinical family trees. Follow these simple steps to start:

1

Click Male (Add) or Female (Add) in the legend at the bottom of the screen to spawn a member.

2

Type their name in the Edit panel that pops up and click Save Changes.

3

Use the shortcuts inside the Edit panel to quickly assign partners/children and connect them.

💡 Or load a template: Select a preloaded case study from the Template dropdown in the top bar to see examples immediately.
